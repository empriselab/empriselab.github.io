RCareWorld Hackathon

In association with EmPRISE, Big Red APDI will be hosting an RCareWorld hackathon this April. RCareWorld is a human-centric simulation world for physical and social robot caregiving with support for realistic human modeling, home environments with multiple levels of accessibility, and robots for assistance.
